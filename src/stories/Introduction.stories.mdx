# Introduction

`react-responsive-slider` is a carousel-style slider that supports CSS-based
responsive designs, multiple items per slide (based on your CSS) and has
built-in lightbox support (using [react-modal][1]) with pinch-zoom. It uses
[useGesture][2] under the hood for mouse/touch gestures and [react-spring][3]
for animations.

[1]: https://reactcommunity.org/react-modal/
[2]: https://use-gesture.netlify.app/
[3]: https://react-spring.io/
